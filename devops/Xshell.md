### 编辑器配色

<img src="../.vuepress/public/Xshell/image-20200504021150611.png" alt="image-20200504021150611" style="zoom:80%;" />

保存文件名位 `skycolor.xcs`

```shell
[skycolor]
text=00ff80
cyan(bold)=00ffff
text(bold)=e9e9e9
magenta=c000c0
green=80ff00
green(bold)=3c5a38
background=042028
cyan=00c0c0
red(bold)=ff0000
yellow=c0c000
magenta(bold)=ff00ff
yellow(bold)=ffff00
red=ff4500
white=c0c0c0
blue(bold)=1e90ff
white(bold)=fdf6e3
black=000000
blue=00bfff
black(bold)=808080
[Names]
name0=skycolor
count=1
```
